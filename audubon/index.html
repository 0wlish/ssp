Invasive Plant Management

Invasive plants damage local ecosystems by crowding out native plants, which distrupts food chains. At Habitat, I helped keep these species at bay through weeding. However, non-native does not equal invasive! There were plenty of non-native plants that we did not pull because they weren't harming the ecosystem.
